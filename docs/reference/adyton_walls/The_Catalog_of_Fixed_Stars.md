# The Catalog of Fixed Stars
> *"The Points of Light from which the Mythos hangs."*

This catalog defines the **104 Fixed Stars** (Coordinates) of the Adyton system.
Unlike the Constellations (which are interpretative forms), these Stars are fixed mathematical points.
They possess unique Gematria (Decimal Values) and act as the structural anchors of the Temple.
