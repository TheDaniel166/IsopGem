# Emerald Tablet Pillar - Guides

<!-- Last Verified: 2025-12-27 -->

**"That which is below is as that which is above."**

This scroll provides instructions for managing the **Library of Correspondences** within the Emerald Tablet.

---

## üìú How to Create a New Tablet

1.  Launch the **Emerald Tablet Hub**.
2.  Click **"New Scroll"**.
3.  **Define Size**: Enter the number of rows and columns you require for your research.
4.  **Name the Work**: Provide a title for your dedicated tablet (e.g., "777 Correspondences", "Lapis Philosophy").
5.  **Initialize**: The application will summon a blank spreadsheet-style grid.

---

## üì• How to Import External Wisdom

1.  Click **"Import"** from the Hub.
2.  **Format**: Select an Excel (`.xlsx`) or CSV file from your system.
3.  **Automated Ingestion**: The application will parse the data into the **CorrespondenceTable** model.
4.  **Review**: The new tablet will open automatically for editing and analysis.

---

## üß™ How to Use the Formula Wizard

Automate the connections between disparate data columns.

1.  With a Tablet open, select a cell and open the **Formula Wizard**.
2.  **Define Source**: Choose a column containing text (e.g., *Column A: Hebrew Words*).
3.  **Select Operator**: Choose an automated transformation, such as:
    *   `=GEMATRIA(A1)`: Automatically calculates the value for the word in Column A.
    *   `=ASTRO(A1)`: Retrieves the planetary association for the word/number.
4.  **Apply to Range**: Drag the formula down to automatically calculate correspondences for the entire column.

---

## üîç Linking to the Gematria Pillar

1.  Highlight a word in your Tablet.
2.  Select **"Send to Gematria"** from the context menu.
3.  The word will be transmitted via the **Signal Bus** to the Gematria Pillar, automatically opening the **Logos Abacus** with the word loaded for analysis.
