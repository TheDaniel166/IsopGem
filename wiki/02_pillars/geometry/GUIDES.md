1.  Open the **3D Geometry Window**.
2.  **Select Solid**: Choose from Tetrahedron, Cube, Octahedron, Dodecahedron, or Icosahedron.
3.  **Rotation & Zoom**: Use the mouse to rotate the object in 3-dimensional space.
4.  **Vertex Analysis**: Hover over vertices to see their spatial coordinates and their relationship to the central point (The Bindu).
5.  **Duality View**: Turn on the "Dual" overlay to see the dual solid (e.g., Cube within an Octahedron) manifesting within the primary form.
