## üìñ How to Perform a Basic Calculation

Use the **Logos Abacus** to transmute text into number.

1.  Launch the **Gematria Hub** from the sidebar.
2.  Click **"Open Abacus"** (Gematria Calculator).
3.  **Input Text**: Type your word or phrase into the central field.
4.  **Select Ciphers**: Use the checkboxes to select the systems you wish to use (e.g., *English Ordinal*, *Hebrew Standard*, *Greek Isopsephy*).
5.  **Review Breakdown**: The lower panel will show a letter-by-letter breakdown of the values.
6.  **Save the Work**: Click the "Disk" icon or press `Ctrl+S` to record the calculation into the **Records of Karnak**.

## üîç How to Search for Numerical Siblings

Find other words or phrases that share the same numerical value.

1.  Launch the **Saved Calculations** window.
2.  **Filter by Value**: Enter a specific number (e.g., `133`) into the "Value" filter.
3.  **Filter by Method**: Select the specific cipher system if you wish to narrow the results.
4.  **Review Matches**: The list will update with every inscribed entry that shares that value.

## ‚õìÔ∏è How to Search for Equidistant Letter Sequences (ELS)

Discover hidden patterns in larger texts using **The Resonant Chain**.

1.  Launch **The Resonant Chain** from the Gematria Hub.
2.  **Select Document**: Choose a manuscript (e.g., *Genesis 1*) from the source list.
3.  **Define Target**: Enter the word you are looking for (e.g., "ELOHIM").
4.  **Set Skip Ranges**: Define the minimum and maximum "Skip" (e.g., skips of 1 to 50).
5.  **Execute**: Click "Begin Search".
6.  **Visualize**: Once a sequence is found, double-click it to view the **ELS Grid**, showing how the sequence wraps through the text.

## üóÇÔ∏è How to Perform Batch Calculations

Transmute a list of words or an entire document at once.

1.  Open the **Batch Calculator**.
2.  **Paste List**: Enter one word per line into the input area.
3.  **Select Primary Cipher**: Choose the system that will govern the calculation.
4.  **Calculate All**: Process the entire list.
5.  **Export**: Save the results as a CSV for use in the **Emerald Tablet** (Correspondences).
