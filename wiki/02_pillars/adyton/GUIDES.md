## üé® How to Design Temple Walls

Customize the inscriptions upon the sanctuary walls based on your research.

1.  Open the **Wall Designer** from the Adyton Hub.
2.  **Select Wall**: Choose North, South, East, or West.
3.  **Inscribe Text**: Enter the words or numbers you wish to manifest.
4.  **Gematria Overlay**: Enable "Gematria Highlighting" to color specific words on the wall based on their numerical value.
5.  **Apply to Sanctuary**: Click "Manifest Changes" to see your inscriptions appear in the 3D sanctuary in real-time.
