## The Ternary-Music Bridge

### Why 12 Semitones?
The fifth (ratio **3/2**) is the most consonant interval after the octave. Stacking 12 fifths nearly equals 7 octaves:

```
(3/2)^12 ≈ 2^7
129.75 ≈ 128
```

This near-closure is why music divides octaves into **12 semitones**.

### Why 8 Octaves?
Human hearing spans ~10 octaves, but **perceptual resolution** is ~8 octaves.

```
8 = 3² - 1
```

The number of bigram intervals equals the number of musical octaves.

### Why 91 Ditrunes Per Octave?

```
(3⁶ - 1) / (3² - 1) = 728 / 8 = 91
91 = 3⁰ + 3² + 3⁴ = 1 + 9 + 81
```

The ternary geometric series quotient equals one octave of ditrunes.
